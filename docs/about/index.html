About AI Resume Maker

Smart resume creation powered by on-device intelligence and simple workflows.

What is AI Resume Maker?

AI Resume Maker is a lightweight resume builder that helps you create professional resumes quickly. It uses on-device intelligence and optional integrations to suggest improvements and produce well-formatted resume documents.

Key features

  • Guided resume templates — start from a clean, professional layout.
  • AI-assisted content suggestions—generate role-specific bullet points and summaries.
  • Local-first storage — resume data is stored locally on your device by default.
  • Ad-supported free version with Google AdMob integrations (configurable).
  • Export to PDF and share via native device share sheet.

How it works

Enter your details (experience, education, skills). Use the AI suggestions to refine wording and structure. Preview and export a PDF when you’re finished. The AI processing happens on-device or via authorized Apple services depending on availability and settings.

AI Resume Maker does not require an account — all resume data remains local unless you explicitly export or share it.

Supported platforms

Designed primarily for mobile devices (iOS / iPadOS). The site can be used as a reference for app information and privacy links.

Integrations

  • Apple Intelligence — optional on-device or Apple-managed processing for text suggestions.
  • Google AdMob — ad serving and analytics for the ad-supported version.
