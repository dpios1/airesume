Privacy Policy

CareerKit – Resume Builder

Overview

CareerKit respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the CareerKit mobile application.

No User Accounts

CareerKit does not require user registration and does not create user accounts. The app can be used without signing in.

Information You Enter

You may choose to enter personal and professional details such as your name, contact information, work experience, education, and skills.

All resume data is stored locally on your device. CareerKit does not store this information on external servers.

Apple Intelligence

CareerKit uses Apple Intelligence to assist with resume generation. Data is processed on-device or via Apple-managed services.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

CareerKit uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect device and ad interaction data according to its privacy policy.

View Google Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

CareerKit does not sell or trade personal information. Data may be shared only with Apple Intelligence, Google AdMob, or when required by law.

Your Control

You can edit or delete resume content at any time, limit ad tracking via device settings, or uninstall the app to remove all stored data.
